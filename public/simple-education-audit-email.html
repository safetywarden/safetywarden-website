Health & Safety Audit for Schools & Colleges
Parents, boards and regulators expect campuses to be safe, compliant and resilient. New Indian regulations—from the OSH Code 2020 to the DPDP Act 2023—demand structured, documented safety practices.

Standards & Codes Covered

  • OSH Code 2020 – employer duties, safety committees, occupational health
  • National Building Code 2016 (Part 4) – fire & life safety
  • CBSE / ISC / UGC Guidelines – education safety norms
  • State Fire NOC – pre-NOC and renewal
  • RPwD Act 2016 – accessibility & inclusion
  • Cybersecurity & Data Privacy – DPDP Act 2023 readiness
  • Communicable Disease Preparedness – infection control & isolation planning
